Privacy Notice

Last updated: March 2026

Gaelg AI is a research platform developed at the University of Sheffield providing Manx Gaelic language technology tools. This notice explains what happens to data you submit when using these tools.

How data is used

Submitted text and audio is sent to our server to be processed solely to generate the requested output (synthesised speech, transcription, or translation). We do not store, log, or retain your input data or the outputs generated beyond the duration of your session. We do not use your data to train or fine-tune models, and we do not share it with third parties.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not use cookies or any form of tracking or analytics.

Data controller

This platform is operated by a researcher at the University of Sheffield. The University of Sheffield's full privacy policy is available at sheffield.ac.uk.

Contact

If you have any questions about how your data is handled, please contact: csjbartley1@sheffield.ac.uk
