# Introducing the Gaelg AI blog

Welcome to the Gaelg AI blog. This is where I'll post **project updates**, notes on new releases, and the occasional thought on building AI tools for a low-resource, critically endangered language.

## What to expect

- Progress on the Speak, Listen, Translate, and Timestamp tools
- Notes on the data and models behind them
- Ways the Manx-speaking community can get involved

The best way to support Manx is to [learn the language](https://www.learnmanx.com/).

*Gura mie ayd* — thanks for reading.
